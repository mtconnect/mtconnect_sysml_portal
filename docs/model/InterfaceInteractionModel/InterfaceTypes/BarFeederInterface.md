# BarFeederInterface

**Supertype:** `Interface`
## Version Info

{: .auto-width }
| Introduced | Deprecated | Updated |"

| 1.3 |  |   |

[`Interface`]({% link model/InterfaceInteractionModel/Interface.md %}) that coordinates the operations between a bar feeder and another piece of equipment.

Bar feeder is a piece of equipment that pushes bar stock (i.e., long pieces of material of various shapes) into an associated piece of equipment – most typically a lathe or turning center.
